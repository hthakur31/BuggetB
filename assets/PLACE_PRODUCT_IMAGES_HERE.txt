Product Preview Images
======================

Save your product preview/screenshot images here:

1. Aptitude Mastery Pack:
   - Save your Google Drive screenshot showing the placement material folders as: aptitude-preview.jpg
   - Path: g:\BuggetB\assets\aptitude-preview.jpg
   - This will display as the product thumbnail for the Aptitude Mastery Pack

You can add more product images by:
1. Adding an 'img' field to the product in index.html (line ~140)
2. Saving the image in this assets folder
3. The image will automatically display as the product thumbnail

Recommended image specs:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Dimensions: 400x300px or similar aspect ratio
- File size: Keep under 500KB for fast loading
